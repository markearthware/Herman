The adventures of two cheeky chaps...

The Tales of Herman and Spice follow two cheeky chaps through a series of adventures that see them saving PC Pudding, discovering a new friend, Zac, in the mountains, and feeding a very hungry lion lots of corned beef!

Aimed at a younger audience but enjoyed by the adults narrating them, The Tales of Herman and Spice make for the perfect bedtime story.
